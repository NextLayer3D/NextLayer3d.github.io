How to Add Your Projects

To add new projects to your gallery:

  1. Upload your project images to a hosting service (Google Photos, Imgur, or your own server)
  2. Copy the image URL (right-click image ‚Üí "Copy image address")
  3. Duplicate one of the gallery items below in the HTML code
  4. Replace the src attribute with your image URL
  5. Update the title, description, material, and layer height
  6. Change the alt text to describe your project

Tip: For best results, use images that are 600x400 pixels or similar aspect ratio (3:2).
